Traci functions used in RL-ODVFC
----------------------------------
Observations: 
1) Current road segment and position of the VFNs and users
traci.vehicle.subscribe(vehID, (tc.VAR_ROAD_ID, tc.VAR_LANEPOSITION))
traci.vehicle.getSubscriptionResults(vehID)

2) Real-time speed from detector 
traci.inductionloop.getLastStepMeanSpeed(loopID)

Action:
1) Judge whether the VFN should take action
current_route = traci.vehicle.getRoute(vehID)
current_edge = traci.vehicle.getRoadID(vehID)
if current_edge == current_route[-1]:
	take action;

2) Set the new edge for the VFNs
traci.vehicle.changeTarget(vehID, edgeID)
